# Task 4 — Investment Strategies
**File:** `task4_investment_strategies.ipynb`

## Goals
- Propose multiple trading strategies. AT LEAST ONE must be a full portfolio
  strategy trading all assets simultaneously.
- Include at least one passive (Buy & Hold) and at least one active strategy.
- Compare strategies using the Sharpe ratio; use a fixed 3% annual interest rate
  as the risk-free benchmark.
- Evaluate how well each strategy is expected to perform in a live/production
  setting (out-of-sample evaluation).
- Strategies may be from the literature (e.g. moving average crossover, channel
  breakout, buy dips) or original.
